Who we are

Empowering IT Professionals Since 1957

At NCOT, we're on a mission to ensure everyone's experience with technology is positive. We're a community of over 70,000 members across 150 countries, committed to making IT good for society.

our vision

To be a global leader in championing the responsible and ethical use of technology for the benefit of all.

our mission

To lead the IT industry through its ethical challenges, support the people who work in the industry, and make IT good for society.
