Who we are

Empowering IT Professionals Since 1957

At NCOT, we're on a mission to ensure everyone's experience with technology is positive. We're a community of over 70,000 members across 150 countries, committed to making IT good for society.

our vision

To be a global leader in championing the responsible and ethical use of technology for the benefit of all.

our mission

To lead the IT industry through its ethical challenges, support the people who work in the industry, and make IT good for society.

learn more

What We Do

Our Strategic Pillars

Membership

We support our members to gain the skills, expertise and connections they need to develop their career and shape the digital future.

learn more

Inspiration

We influence and improve computing education at all levels to inspire the next generation of IT professionals and create a diverse talent pipeline.

learn more

Progression

We provide opportunities for learning and development to support people's career progression and assess talent through a diverse range of qualifications.

learn more

Influence

We tackle the big issues facing our digital lives, consulting industry stakeholders and policymakers to bring about impactful change on society and our profession.
